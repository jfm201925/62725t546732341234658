About and discography

Jayface (Akos Juhasz) is an independent electronic music producer and DJ, working across various genres including trance, house, techno, and electro. It's been 20 years since he created the name Jayface back in 2005 and started writing music and registering it under that name. To celebrate the 20th anniversary of the name’s creation, he launched a dedicated merch subsite called Jayface Shop. His released albums so far: Drifting Worlds Album (2025), Meet Her At The Club Album (2023), The Album (2022), and Summer Trance Dreams Album (2009). Jayface is also the founder, label owner, and creative director of Jayface Music, as well as the curator and host of the Jayface Radio Show. Every creative aspect of his brand - from producing and mixing tracks, to crafting visuals, managing distribution, running social platforms, and overseeing the label’s direction - is handled entirely by him. With a fully independent and hands-on approach, Jayface builds his musical universe from the ground up. Every track released, every visual identity, and every brand element reflects his vision and artistic integrity. In a world that often divides creative roles, Jayface chooses to do it all - and does it with precision and purpose. Jayface’s music has achieved significant international recognition, with the single Let Go reaching number 1 on the Mexico iTunes Trance chart and top 5 positions in India’s dance charts. Multiple tracks have appeared on prominent Beatport trance compilations, including The Green Valley and The Tree Of Life: Beginnings, featured in the Closing and After Hours Essentials 2024 playlists. Additionally, Jayface’s tracks have been played on various global radio shows, notably Solarstone’s Pure Trance Radio, where his work has received special acclaim - as Solarstone himself described, "This fabulous uplifter was an ID in Solarstone's sets for months, but is finally yours to have!"
